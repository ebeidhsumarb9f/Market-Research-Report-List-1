<p><strong>The Semiconductor Intellectual Property Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard IP Cores</li><li>Soft IP Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Intellectual Property (SIP) market comprises two main types: Hard IP Cores and Soft IP Cores. Hard IP Cores are pre-designed, fixed-function blocks that are delivered as fully manufactured chips, providing reliable performance and lower design risks. In contrast, Soft IP Cores are more flexible, typically delivered in the form of synthesizable code, allowing designers to modify and optimize them for specific applications. Both types cater to various needs in chip design, enhancing efficiency and innovation in semiconductor development.</p></p>
