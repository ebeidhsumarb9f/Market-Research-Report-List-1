<p><p>The musculoskeletal diseases treatment market encompasses various imaging modalities crucial for diagnosing and managing conditions. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed images of soft tissues, making it excellent for assessing cartilage, ligaments, and muscles. Computed Tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional views of bones and is useful for detecting fractures and complex bone structures. Ultrasound provides real-time imaging of soft tissues and is often used for guided injections. Other methods include X-rays and advanced imaging techniques, enhancing diagnosis and treatment planning for musculoskeletal issues.</p></p>
